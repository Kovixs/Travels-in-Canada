Toronto

If one would expect to witness the multiple cultures as well as fun-filled adventures, then head to Toronto which is a conspicuous gateway for a perfect holiday. One can seek the most fun activities as it is an epic center for music, movies as well as filming productions.

Peggy's Cove

Peggy's Cove is one of the popular Tourist places in Canada for its breathtaking views and picturesque landscape. This place is composed of some local fishing communities making it a significant place. It has one of the popular attractions, Peggy's Point, that owns a red and white lighthouse.

Toronto's zoo

Stretching for approximately 287 hectares, Toronto Zoo is the largest zoo in Canada. The zoo is divided into seven parts representing seven geographic regions namely Indo-Malaya, Americas, Africa, Tundra Trek, Eurasia, Australasia, and Canadian Domain.

Niagara falls

One of the naturally held stunning waterfalls of the World, Niagara falls attracts a large number of visitors every year with it's aesthetic scenes. There's also a large number of amenities facilities while wandering around the falls like watching the whole scene of waterfalls from the observation towers.

Hockey hall of fame

Hockey Hall of Fame is a museum that is located in Toronto that is dedicated to the history of ice hockey. It is also regarded as a home for the Stanley Cup and the finest collections of Hockey artefacts in the entire world. This alone makes a wonderful destination to stop by.

CN tower

CN tower is the tallest free standing telecommunications tower in Canada, North America and the western hemisphere. This tower has been one of the seven wonders of the modern world since 1995. It happens to be a 553.2 meter tall tower and it is named as CN which refers to Canadian National, the company who constructed it.
